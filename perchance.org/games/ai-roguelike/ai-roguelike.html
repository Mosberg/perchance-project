Info

Keybinds:

  • Arrow Keys/WASD: move
  • Q: Inspect
  • R: Show map
  • F: Next Turn

Instructions:

  • To attack an entity, move into it.
  • Inspecting an entity lets you interact with them using ai.
  • You can click on the grid to move towards cursor
  • You can use Arrow Keys/WASD to "click" on the entity standing on that side of you
  • convince a creature to come with you and they will follow (or aleast should, this feature is still WIP)

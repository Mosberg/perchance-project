Welcome to Interactive RPG Creator!

This tool allows you to create your own interactive RPG story with custom characters and backgrounds. Craft engaging narratives and bring your imagination to life!

Tips for the best experience:

  • Make sure to make the characters and lore descriptive for a richer story.
  • This is not a perfect generator, feel free to provide your feedbacks.
  • Visit Perchance Chatbot and New AI Chat for simpler interface.
  • The character sprites should preferably be png with transparent background.
  • Be creative with your story elements to create unique experiences.
  • You can customize each character's emotions for more expressive interactions.
  • Visit AI Image Generator to AI-generate characters.
  • For same character with different emotions, make sure to keep the seed *same* and the description *similar*.
  • Try using detailed prompts when describing your characters for more consistent results.
  • Consider creating complementary characters that will interact well together in your story.

Ready to create your own adventure? Click the close button to get started!
